# Credits and Monetization

# Summary

- The platform uses a **credit** economy: credits pay for **creating games** and for **funding ongoing changes** (bugs/features/balance/content).
- Revenue comes from **creators paying to create** and from **players/creators buying credits** to spend on evolving games.

# Goals

- Make ongoing evolution economically sustainable for the platform and aligned with real demand.
- Keep pricing understandable: **pay for creation** and **pay for impact** (funded work).

# In Scope

- **Credits purchase** and **credit balances** for spending.
- **Spend** on game creation and on **request funding** (and engine cost differences where applicable).
- Two-sided revenue: **creation fees** + **ongoing credit purchases/spend**.
